ABOUT THE TECHNOLOGY ENTREPRENEURSHIP CLUB

TEC is a student-run organization at the University of Pennsylvania. Our goal is to bring out the entrepreneur within us and foster student collaboration to spread our passion for technology and entrepreneurship to the UPenn campus.



Engage

We strive to connect students with start-up companies and other students for potential collaboration.

Empower:

We provide guidance to students so that they can create their own start up company.

Envision:

We provide guidance and consulting for students who wish to create their own start up company.

As a club, we function through our committees. The committees are a great way to get more involved at TEC. Through committees you get the chance to influence the direction our club takes, and directly work on different sectors within the club.
